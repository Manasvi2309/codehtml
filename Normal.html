About House

A house is a building that is made for people to live in. It is usually built for a family (parents and their children). It is a "permanent" building that is meant to stay standing. It is not easily packed up and carried away like a tent, or moved like a caravan.




A house serves as a primary shelter and living space, providing protection from the elements and a place for people to reside, cook, eat, sleep, and perform other daily activities.
